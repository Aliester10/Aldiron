About Aldiron Jaya Makmur

PT. Aldiron Jaya Makmur was established on September 2, 2022, as a logistics and cargo delivery company headquartered in Cikarang, Bekasi. We provide nationwide shipping services across Indonesia through air, land, and sea transportation.

As one of the growing domestic expedition companies, we offer Express, ONS, Regular, Trucking, and Container services at competitive prices while maintaining speed, reliability, and safety.

Supported by professional human resources and well-organized warehouse facilities, AJM ensures efficient and secure logistics operations to meet customer demands. We are committed to delivering trusted solutions that create value for our clients and support Indonesia's logistics growth.
