Introduction Aldiron Jaya Makmur

PT Aldiron Jaya Makmur is a company established in November 1, 2005 specialized in Logistics and Freight Forwarding services. Our commitment is to provide excellent service and build long-term relationships with our clients.

We take pride in our exceptional logistics and transportation services that have been trusted by many clients. Tracking, freight, warehousing, and distribution – we handle it all. With a team of experienced professionals, we ensure every shipment arrives safely and on time.

Our mission is to be the leading logistics provider in Indonesia, offering innovative solutions and maintaining the highest standards of service quality. We continuously invest in technology and infrastructure to better serve our customers.
